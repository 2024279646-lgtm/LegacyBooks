Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting your preferred book and clicking the "Add To Cart" button.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit Card, Debit Card, Online Banking and E-Wallet.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 3 to 7 working days within Malaysia.

4. Can I return a book after purchase?

Returns are only accepted for damaged or incorrect items.

5. How can I contact Legacy Books?

You can contact us through email, phone number, or WhatsApp listed on the website.
