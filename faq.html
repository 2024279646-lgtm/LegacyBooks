Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about ordering, payment, shipping and customer support at Legacy Books.

1. How can I place an order?

You can place an order by selecting your preferred book and clicking the "Add To Cart" button or "Buy Now" button.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Credit Card, Debit Card, Online Banking and selected digital payment options.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping usually takes 3 to 7 working days within Malaysia depending on your delivery location.

4. Can I return a book after purchase?

Returns are accepted only for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact us as soon as possible after receiving your order.

5. How can I contact Legacy Books?

You can contact us through email, phone number or WhatsApp for any enquiries regarding orders, delivery or book availability.

6. Do you offer promotions or discounts?

Yes, we occasionally offer promotions, bundle deals and seasonal discounts for selected books and stationery items.
